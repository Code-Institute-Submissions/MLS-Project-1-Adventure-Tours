About Us

OUR MISSION STATEMENT

We aim to provide for your every need, and give you the best Zimbabwean experience of your lifetime. We want you to become familiar with the place that we call ‘home’. It is the place where we grew up and hold so many cherished memories close to our hearts.

Why travel with Us

Adventure Tours provides you with the best camping sites and hiking sports around the country. We are here to help you quickly and easily find attractions in Zimbabwe for that perfect holiday.As a traveller, You have never been satisfied with what is in front of you. You have always wanted to see what is around the corner, over the next hill. And, that is what we do — we introduce you to a destination in a way that lets you experience it fully. We’ll show you the attractions, and then the attractions beyond the attractions, and give you the perspective to feel like you are not just visiting a place, you are a part of it.

Our specialists have all traveled extensively in their regions and they return each year to learn more to find new and different ways for you to experience your destination. That means that we can arrange immersive tours and off-the-beaten-path activities to help you get at the authentic soul of a place. We also work with the best local guides and can suggest the most interesting places for you to stay.
